When creating a world, you will be placed on a random world filled with islands
Gather resources on these islands by destroying them with your left mouse button
Place gathered blocks with your right mouse button
See your items in your inventory by pressing E
Craft new items in the crafting menu by pressing t
All recipes for which you have sufficient resources will be displayed
Get access to further recipes by using a crafting table
At night monsters will come! Gear Up accordingly
Damage enemies with your left mouse button
